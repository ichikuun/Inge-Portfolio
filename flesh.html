flesh

Medium: 1-minute film
Timeline: January 2017
Role: Co-Director & Director of Photography
Synopsis: A young man confronts his stalker. A chef cooks his dinner. The predator pounces on his prey. It all connects.

This film was submitted for the Film Riot One Minute Short Film Competition by Filmstro, a soundtrack editing program.

The magic of the looks is thanks to two Aputure LED lights and a mechanical lens, the Voigtlander 40mm, with the amazing color rendering.

Working with food is quite challenging because we can't un-cook the dish to shoot again or un-eat the meat to refilm the eating scene.
