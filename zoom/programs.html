PROGRAMS

Jump Acrobat Trampoline Classes

Want to learn different trampoline skills and routines? Learn how to do a Pike Jump, Seat Drop, Back Drop and the foundation skills in the sport of trampoline.

A recreational “Learn to Trampoline” Program based on the Long Term Trampoline Athlete Development Model released by Gymnastics Canada. The program was developed by Louise Stack, Australian National Team Athlete and Level 3 Canadian Coach.

At Zoom Park Singapore, the program will be led by Jessica Hinton, British Senior Club Coach and National Competitor along with our Jump Acrobat Certified Instructors.

Learn how to master the foundations of the sport of Trampoline:
Shaped Jumps, Seat, Back and Front Landings, Swivel Hips, Full Twists and The Cradle.

Zoom Kids

Can your little one walk? If they can walk, they can jump! Our Zoom Kids are available every Wednesday and Thursday from 10am to noon where we reserve certain sections of our Trampoline Park just for the little ones. They can jump, bounce and crawl around with their friends and parent partner!

Our court monitors will ensure that the children are having a ZOOMing safe time at our park! The high performance mats will be out of bounds for the young kids. During Zoom Kids, parents (or legal guardian) are allowed to join their child for free (only 1 parent per child is free).

AGE GROUP:
No age limit! Parents would need to supervise the children.

TIME:
Tuesday, Thursday, Friday: 10am - 3pm
Saturday and Sunday: 9am - 1pm

PRICE:
Non-Peak Days (Tuesday and Thursday) – $13 per child per hour
Peak Days (Friday to Sunday) – $16 per child per hour
* Parents come in for free in all sessions.

Zoom Night

Come on Friday and Saturday nights and enjoy our Zoom Night. On Zoom Night, we will be dimming the lights and playing the latest hit songs to create a disco experience on our trampoline courts. On our special disco nights, we sell glow stick bracelets to amplify your experience!

Churn out your best dance moves with your friends in a non-alcohol environment and end your Friday/Saturday night with a bang!

AGE GROUP:
Limited to Age 15 and above
For toddlers and younger children who wants to join, parents must accompany them in to supervise

TIME:
7pm to closing every Friday and Saturday

PRICE:
Just pay your regular admission fee and join in our fun!

Baby Bounce

Class Registration is open! Email jumpacrobat@zoomparkasia.com to register now!
Baby Bounce features instructor-led trampoline lessons targeted to toddlers and parents. You can accompany your child and learn the basics of trampoline together. The skills taught in each session are combined with play and games to enhance learning and make it more engaging and entertaining.

Sessions are excellent for early development of spatial awareness in toddlers, as well as great developoment of motor skills and body control. It has been proven to improve coordination and movement, and is known to be a low-impact exercise perfect for toddlers and young children.

AGE GROUP:
2 - 4 Year-Olds

TIME:
11am to 12noon every Wednesday

PRICE:
$25 per pair (for 1 parent and 1 child)
* Maximum class size is 15 pairs. Call in and book early to avoid disappointment.
