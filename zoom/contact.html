Directions

By Public Transport:
From Jurong East MRT station, take Bus 51, 78 or 143 and drop off at Opp Blk 2 along Jurong Town Hall Road. Head south on Jurong Town Hall Rd toward Pandan Gardens.

By Car:
Drivers can easily get off at AYE Exit 13 and turn towards Jurong Town Hall Road, before turning left into Pandan Gardens.
