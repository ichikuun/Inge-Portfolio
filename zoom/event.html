EVENTS

Birthday Parties

Zoom Park Singapore is the ultimate birthday party venue in Singapore with guaranteed fun! Throw the party of the year with your family and friends in our party rooms. Our friendly and enthusiastic staff will help you ensure that your party is fun and unforgettable!
Bring the party to the trampoline courts with jumping, flying and flipping for an hour! You can also play your favorite sports, such as Extreme Dodgeball, Slam Dunk and even Rock wall climbing.
Your party guests – and the guest of honor – will be greeted at the reception by our Event Host, who will provide all party jumpers with grip socks and wrist bands. After 60 minutes of jumping on our trampoline court, proceed on to your party room for food and drinks!

Reserve today and see your party guests smile!

Terms and Conditions:
Party package bookings require a 50% deposit.
Room bookings require deposits equivalent to room booking charges.
Deposits must be made at least 7 days in advance.
Final payment must be made on date of party.

To find out more about the booking for event at Zoom Park; or to discuss additional catering requirements, please contact us at info@zoomparkasia.com or speak to our events coordinator @ +65-6334 4615.

School Events

Kids are full of energy! What better way to burn the energy off than jumping around in our Zoom trampoline park? Zoom is the epitome of fun and freedom and a fantastic venue to host your school events. Create a truly memorable experience for your students! Zoom Trampoline Park boasts about 20,000 square feet of indoor fun space and ten trampoline activity areas! We have options for your school or your class to enjoy jumping here at Zoom!

ADD-ONS:
• Finger foods starting from $8 per pax (menu provided via email)
• Grip socks – $2 per pair

Terms and Conditions:
Event must be scheduled 2 weeks in advance.
50% deposit is due at the time of event reservation.
All jumpers require a signed liability waiver. Those under age 18 are required to have a signed waiver from a parent or legal guardian above the age of 21.
Time slots are subject to sellouts. Prices and Hours are subject to change without notice.
Outside food is allowed if full facility is booked.
For booking of only party rooms, no outside food is allowed.

If you are looking at booking of the full facility, please contact us at events@zoomparkasia.com or speak to our events coordinator @ +65-6334-4615! Prices are before GST.
