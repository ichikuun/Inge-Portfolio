About

FMS30 based on 29Rooms, where each experiential room is uniquely themed and Instagram-worthy. FMS30 has 13 rooms about aspects of the craft, like production, journalism and radio. The exhibition is open for 3 days and welcomed all past school directors and over 300 guests on its opening night.

Over 2 months, I led a team of volunteer alumni and current students to design the space and themes of each room, taking into consideration the showcased material and client needs. We also sourced for logistics to bring each themed room to life and worked closely with the supervising lecturer to negotiate with the school board and contractors.
