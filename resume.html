About Me

My name is Inge Handoko Shi.

I am a photographer, a writer, a videographer, a designer, a coder and many more. I have many skillsets, many hobbies and even more dreams. Wherever I am, I always try to learn as much as possible so that I can achieve every single one of my lofty dreams.

I knew what I wanted to do since I was 12, when I first stepped into a local radio station in Indonesia and recorded an astonishingly bad radio segment. I gazed in awe at all the sound technicians, producers and other radio DJs the whole time I was there. Even with my overactive imagination, I could see no other future for me than being in the media industry.

Years since then, I have honed my skills in writing, design and production so that I would have a well-equipped toolkit to tackle whatever problems I would meet. It also helped that I am adventurous and love learning new things. From the uses of weird gadgets to new programming languages, my thirst for new knowledge cannot be quenched.

Life, for me, is an adventure and discovery of exciting new things. Whatever comes tomorrow is another episode to each of our unique journeys.
