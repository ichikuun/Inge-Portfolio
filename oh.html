FMS Open House 2018

Medium: Outdoor Print
Client: School of Film & Media Studies
Timeline: October 2017 - January 2018
About: I was part of a creative team tasked to conceptualise and design for my school's annual open house exhibition, as well as liaise with the contractors to make the magic happen.

The FMS foyer was the venue for the exhibition and where many of the open house activities took place at.

Top: The information booth follows specific dimensions to allow FMS Agents, the ambassadors of the school, to host visitors.

Left: The final artwork for the exhibition was created by the contractors' design team and we fitted the key visual into templates for printing.
